# Privacy Policy for Fit It

**Last Updated: April 11, 2026**

Fit It ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Fit It mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use our application.

## 1. Information We Collect
We believe in your privacy. Fit It is designed to be a local utility tool.
*   **No Personal Data:** We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
*   **No Content Tracking:** The images you process or text you generate remain entirely on your device. We do not upload your content to any servers.
*   **Technical Data:** We may collect basic, anonymous technical data (e.g., app crashes or performance logs) via standard platform services (like Google Play Console) solely to improve app stability.

## 2. Permissions
The app requests minimum permissions to function:
*   **Storage Access:** Required ONLY to read images you choose to edit and save resulting images back to your gallery.
*   **Internet Access:** Required ONLY for standard crash reporting and if required by future feature integrations (currently unused for user data).

## 3. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services like Google Play Services that collect information used to identify you in accordance with their own policies.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
